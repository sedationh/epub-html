CONCLUSION
结论

As I said at the outset, my goal is to pass along the principles that worked well for me; what you do with them is up to you.
正如我一开始所说，我的目标是传递对我有用的原则;你怎么处理它们取决于你自己。

I of course hope that they will help you visualize your own audacious goals, navigate through your painful mistakes, have quality reflections, and come up with good principles of your own that you will systematically follow to produce outcomes that vastly exceed your expectations. I hope that they will help you do these things both individually and when working with others. And, since your journey and evolution will certainly be a struggle, I hope that these principles will help you struggle and evolve well. Perhaps they will even inspire you and others to put your principles in writing and collectively figure out what’s best in an idea-meritocratic way. If I could tilt the world even one degree more in that direction, that would thrill me.
我当然希望他们能帮助你想象你自己的大胆目标，克服你的痛苦错误，进行高质量的反思，并提出你自己的好原则，你将系统地遵循这些原则，以产生大大超出你预期的结果。我希望他们能帮助你单独和与他人合作时做这些事情。而且，由于你的旅程和进化肯定会是一场斗争，我希望这些原则能帮助你奋斗和进化。也许它们甚至会激励你和其他人把你的原则写下来，并以一种理念精英的方式集体找出最好的方法。如果我能让世界向那个方向倾斜哪怕一度，那会让我兴奋不已。

Along these lines, there is more to come. Because I know that having tools and protocols is necessary to helping people convert what they want to do into actually doing it, I will soon be making the ones we’ve created available to you.
沿着这些思路，还有更多即将到来。因为我知道拥有工具和协议对于帮助人们将他们想做的事情转化为实际操作是必要的，所以我很快就会将我们创建的工具和协议提供给您。

I feel I have now done the best I can to pass along my Life and Work Principles. Of course, we aren’t done with our struggles until we die. Since my latest struggle has been to pass along whatever I have that has been of value, I feel a certain sense of relief to have gotten these principles out to you, and a sense of contentment as I end this book and turn my attention to passing along my economic and investment principles.
我觉得我现在已经尽了最大的努力来传递我的人生和工作原则。当然，直到我们死去，我们的挣扎才算结束。由于我最近的努力是传递我所拥有的有价值的一切，因此将这些原则传达给您时，我感到某种解脱感，并且当我结束这本书并将注意力转向传递我的经济和投资原则时，我感到满足。

ABOUT THE AUTHOR
关于作者

Ray Dalio, who grew up a very ordinary middle-class kid from Long Island, started the investment company Bridgewater Associates out of his two-bedroom apartment when he was 26 years old, and built it over the next 42 years into what Fortune magazine assessed to be the fifth most important private company in the U.S. He did that by creating a unique culture—an idea meritocracy based on radical truth, radical transparency, and believability-weighted decision making—that he believes most people and organizations can use to better achieve their own goals.
雷·达里奥 （Ray Dalio） 在长岛一个非常普通的中产阶级孩子长大，他在 26 岁时在他的两居室公寓里创办了投资公司 Bridgewater Associates，并在接下来的 42 年里将其打造成被《财富》杂志评为美国第五大最重要的私营公司。他通过创造一种独特的文化来做到这一点——一种基于激进真理、激进透明和可信度加权决策的任人唯贤的理念——他相信大多数人和组织都可以利用它来更好地实现自己的目标。

Along the way, Dalio became one of the 100 most influential (according to Time) and 100 wealthiest (according to Forbes) people in the world, and because his unique investment principles changed the industry, CIO magazine called him “the Steve Jobs of investing.” (Those principles will be conveyed in his next book, Economic and Investment Principles.) He believes that his success isn’t due to anything special about him—it is the result of principles he learned, largely by making mistakes, from which he also believes most people can benefit.
一路走来，达里奥成为全球 100 位最具影响力（根据《时代》）和最富有（根据福布斯）的 100 人之一，由于他独特的投资原则改变了行业，CIO 杂志称他为“投资界的史蒂夫乔布斯”。（这些原则将在他的下一本书《经济与投资原则》（Economic and Investment Principles）中阐述。他认为，他的成功并不是因为他有什么特别之处——而是他学到的原则的结果，主要是通过犯错，他也相信大多数人都可以从中受益。

At 68 years old, Dalio’s primary objective is to pass along these principles in case others find them of value.
68 岁的达里奥的主要目标是将这些原则传承下去，以防其他人认为它们有价值。
